

Cameras are controlled by the uvcvideo kernel module.

Disable the camera until reboot - sudo modprobe -r uvcvideo.

If there's the error message: modprobe: FATAL: Module uvcvideo is in use. -  sudo rmmod -f uvcvideo

To enable webcam again - sudo modprobe uvcvideo

Permanently disable webcam.

blacklist uvcvideo >> /etc/modprobe.d/blacklist.conf
